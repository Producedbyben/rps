How this works
  • Every run is 20 rounds, and each round is logged in the unified board with hands plus W/L/D outcome.
  • Dwayne starts from a fixed base script; in Adaptive mode he may try to counter your last move after round 1.
  • His mood shifts from Calm to Suspicious to Paranoid based on recent losses, and mood changes adapt chance.
  • Use the round board hover/focus preview to inspect exact hands, state, and whether the adaptive path was used.
  • Set a seed in the field above to replay exact runs, or leave it blank to randomize next reset.
  • Achievements unlock immediately when earned, stay unlocked for this browser profile, and use color tiers by difficulty.
